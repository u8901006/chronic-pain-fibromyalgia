# Fibromyalgia / Chronic Pain Journal Map and PubMed Search Templates

Curated English reference file for cross-disciplinary literature scouting in fibromyalgia and chronic pain.

## Scope

This file focuses on journals and search terms relevant to **medicine, neuroscience, psychology, sociology, biology, rehabilitation science, and rTMS/neuromodulation** for **fibromyalgia (FM)** and related **chronic widespread / nociplastic pain** research.

## Common Keyword Bank

### Core condition

fibromyalgia, fibromyalgia syndrome, FMS, chronic widespread pain, CWP, nociplastic pain, chronic primary pain

### Symptoms/phenotype

widespread pain, fatigue, sleep disturbance, insomnia, cognitive dysfunction, fibro fog, tenderness, allodynia, hyperalgesia

### Mechanisms

central sensitization, pain modulation, descending inhibition, temporal summation, small fiber neuropathy, neuroinflammation, autonomic dysfunction, HPA axis, brain network, functional connectivity

### Psychology

pain catastrophizing, depression, anxiety, trauma, PTSD, dissociation, somatization, illness perception, pain acceptance, self-efficacy

### Rehabilitation

exercise therapy, aerobic exercise, resistance training, aquatic therapy, graded activity, pacing, pain neuroscience education, multidisciplinary rehabilitation

### Social/behavioral

quality of life, stigma, gender differences, social support, work disability, healthcare utilization, lived experience, qualitative

### Neuromodulation

rTMS, repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation, TMS, transcranial direct current stimulation, tDCS, neuromodulation, motor cortex, DLPFC

### Biology

cytokines, glia, immune dysregulation, mitochondria, oxidative stress, microcirculation, serotonin, dopamine, blood biomarkers

## Notes

- This is a **curated cross-disciplinary journal map**, not a claim that every listed journal is fibromyalgia-specific.
- Use the keyword bank to adapt searches for symptoms, mechanisms, interventions, and social context.
- For pain-neuroscience work, combine **fibromyalgia** with **nociplastic pain**, **central sensitization**, **functional connectivity**, and **pain modulation**.
- For neuromodulation work, combine **fibromyalgia** with **rTMS**, **TMS**, **motor cortex**, **DLPFC**, **tDCS**, and **neuromodulation**.
